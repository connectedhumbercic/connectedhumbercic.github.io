About Us

Our Projects

Humber LoRa

We are working to create a low powered radio network that covers the whole region. This will enable the deployment of low cost sensors that can be the basis of a huge range of applications.

Monitair

The Monitair project is building a network of conncted air quality sensors. We are creating sensor designs, networking infrastructure and visualisation tools to help us understand the local air quality situation.

Build Events

We want to run build events where local people can engage with technology by creating working devices, whether they are building robots to play with or air quality sensors to connect to the Monitair network.
